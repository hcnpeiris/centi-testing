Chapter One

The Beginning

The journey started with four ordinary college students who shared a common dream — the desire to produce distinctive music. Amila, Ishan, Eranga, and Thusith.

They started with cover versions of popular Pop, Ethnic RnB, and Alternative bands. As newcomers, all their performances were highly appreciated by the audience. In 2002, they recorded two studio singles — "Tradition" and "Dark Angel" — to perform at eminent gatherings and college conferences. These up-tempo songs were instant hits and grabbed the immediate attention of some leading media networks in the country, with both songs receiving airplay in 2003.

Chapter Two
2003 – 2008

Rise to Fame

"Tradition" entered the Sri Lankan Hot 40 chart at #2. Their song "Dark Angel" took only 24 hours to jump into ABC Network's Sun FM Hot 40 Countdown — and the very next week it was sitting at #1, competing with major international acts and holding that spot for four straight weeks. The songs even received airplay on BBC Radio 1.

Their 2005 debut album "Heritage" rocketed up the charts, holding the record for the fastest-selling debut album in Sri Lanka. CENTIGRADZ then released a string of groundbreaking music videos — "Pahasara," "Oba Ekwenawado," and "Ranwan Mal Dam" — ruling the video charts for 15 straight weeks in 2008. They also produced theme songs for films "Heart FM" and "Asai Man Piyabanna."

Chapter Three
World Stage

The World Stage

CENTIGRADZ were nominated to represent Sri Lanka at the "Asia Pacific World Star Quest" Finals in Penang, Malaysia — a competition spanning 13 countries. They showed class and true professionalism at the outdoor concert in Auto City, winning the "Excellent New Singer Award."

They went on to win "Most Popular Music Group of the Year" twice in a row, and the State Presidential Music Award for "Best Melody Composition" for their smash hit "Diurala Pawasanna." Having completed over 500 sold-out shows, the guys continue to generate the highest ticket sales in the country — lifted always by the loyalty of fans who adore their unity, simplicity, and undying passion for music.
